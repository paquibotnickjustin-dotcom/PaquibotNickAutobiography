About Me

Hello! My name is Nick Justin B. Paquibot, and this is the story of my life... I am an aspiring IT/Geology student studying here in Lapu-Lapu City College. Now why is there a "/Geology" on that sentence? It's because aside from being an IT student, a piece of me is also interested in Geology, also known as the study of rocks and minerals.


I am currently 18 years old, born here in the City of East, aka Lapu-Lapu City. I consider myself as an adventurous type of student, but aside from that, I am also the type of student who is indecisive. What I noticed about me is that apart from all my strengths, there are always weaknesses that are ready to hinder my strengths.


Now... why am I making this autobiography? As a student, it is our goal to caress and take care of our grades, not just for myself, but for my future. If I want to have a good future, I should start on improving and being the best me. School may sometimes be considered a hindrance on some of our personal stuff, but trust me, here is where we develop different sets of skills that we may not hone on our own, this place is where we prepare for the future.

Education

As a student, one of my goals is to finish school with flying colors, and to finish it with flying colors, I would do good in all activities. Some may call me "smart", but for me I still am not. My mind had already set the meaning of being smart when I studied at STEC. In that school, everything is advanced. At first, it was very fast for me, considering that when I was still studying at MNHS for my JHS it was too chill. And the SHS experience hit me with a culture shock, which made me the person who I am right now.


When I arrived in LLCC, it also hit me with another culture shock, which was the realization of how different college compared with SHS. The first day was rough for me, since I did not know anyone in the class, and I'm glad that after a few weeks, I found new peers and am also comfortable in the new environment.
